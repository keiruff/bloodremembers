• Zulu Kingdom (1816–1897 CE): military & cultural giant.

    • Rupert’s Valley, St. Helena

    A forgotten graveyard of freedom seekers. Between 1840 and 1872, over 25,000 Africans were "liberated" by the British Royal Navy and brought here. Many died in quarantine and were buried in unmarked graves.

    We remember them not as victims—but as ancestors.
