Frequently Asked Questions

Is ancestry DNA testing accurate?

DNA testing is highly accurate for identifying regional origins and connecting with potential relatives. The more specific the database (like African Ancestry), the deeper your insights.

Will my data be safe?

Each company has its own privacy policy. Be sure to read how your data is stored and if it’s shared with third parties.

Can I use this for reparations claims or dual citizenship?

In some cases, yes. DNA evidence has been used in advocacy efforts and applications for ancestral reconnection in certain countries. Check the specific requirements involved.
