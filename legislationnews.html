Reparations are being discussed in legislatures across the country. Here’s a summary of recent developments.

Federal Level

  • H.R. 40: Establishes a commission to study and develop reparations proposals for African Americans. View Bill
  • S. 40: Senate version of H.R. 40, reintroduced by Sen. Cory Booker. View Bill

Washington, D.C.

The city has enacted a law allowing the exploration of reparations for Black residents with ties to slavery and Jim Crow. A 12-member commission will study the city’s history and offer proposals. Learn more

California

The California Legislative Black Caucus introduced 14 bills in 2024 based on the 2023 California Reparations Report. These proposals address wealth gaps, education, healthcare, and more. See details

Key Themes Across the U.S.

  • Cash payments, land grants, and community investments
  • Formal apologies and recognition of systemic harm
  • Ongoing debates about race-based programs and legal precedent
