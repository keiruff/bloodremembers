April 2025 — Healing Through History: Honoring Our Ancestors

In this issue: We explore how reconnecting with our ancestral stories fosters collective healing and strengthens our fight for justice.

  • Historical Reflections: A look into sacred burial grounds, like Rupert’s Valley, and the role of remembrance.
  • Spiritual Reclamation: The power of naming ancestors, honoring lineages, and embracing cultural rituals.
  • Community Healing: Spotlight on grassroots projects preserving Black and Indigenous memory.

Each story is a thread in the fabric of our legacy. This month, we encourage you to light a candle, say their names, and share your family’s stories.

May 2025 — Reparations 101: What Are We Really Owed?

In this issue: Understand what reparations truly mean and why they matter more than ever in 2025.

  • Historical Precedents: Examples of reparations paid—just not to the enslaved.
  • Policy Proposals: A breakdown of H.R. 40 and current legislative momentum.
  • Economic Truths: How stolen labor created generational Black wealth gaps.
  • What You Can Do: Sign the petition, educate others, and support local initiatives.

We are not asking for handouts—we are demanding a long-overdue justice rooted in history and humanity.
