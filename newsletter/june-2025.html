How to Trace Freedmen and Black Native Ancestry

This month, we reflect on the importance of ancestral healing. Whether through storytelling, ceremony, or activism, remembering our ancestors gives us strength and clarity in the present. Learn how your history connects to sacred resistance and survival.

How to Understand Your DNA Report

  • 1. Regions & Ethnic Groups: Your DNA results show trace regions like Yoruba, Bantu, or Mende. These aren't guesses—they're connections to historical populations.
  • 2. Trace Percentages: Even <1% can be meaningful—your ancestors lived in multiple places due to forced migration.
  • 3. Related Communities: Many DNA platforms group you with living relatives or shared ancestry clusters—use those to guide your genealogy search.
